Alternative therapies
Should a patient’s medical condition require more extensive treatment, naturotherapist Sejal Vora engages with experts in alternative therapies that also emphasise mind over matter, e.g. shiatsu (a Chinese system using finger pressure to help activate the natural flow of energy in the nervous system); hypnotherapy (inducing a hypnotic state in the patient to increase motivation or alter a harmful behaviour pattern); homeopathy (including life force healing); Theta, Bach flower remedy and yoga.
This may be recommended in tandem with naturopathy to aid the patient and make the journey as pleasant as possible and with a view to get to the root of a clinical condition and treat it by using non-invasive methods, improve blood circulation and thereby natural healing, to contemplate the inner self and understand the disease, release debilitating emotional stress or all of it together.
